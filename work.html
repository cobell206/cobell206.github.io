Tesla

April - September 2016

Tesla was a great experience where I worked for the Service Engineering team to develop service procedures and service tooling. I first took apart the cars to come up with a quick and inexpensive way to diagnose thermal issues in Model S and X in the case a car comes in with an overheating error. Once finished, I helped develop some tooling to flush the A/C system that's easy to use and portable. Lastly after fishing some other service tooling jobs, I helped look over Model 3 to see if there were any design issues we could induce before release to reduce service costs on the car. These changes were then sent back to the design teams to see if they could incorporate them into their designs.
